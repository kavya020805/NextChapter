# Librarian Interview ‚Äì Resource Center (DAU)

As part of the requirements elicitation process, we conducted an audio interview with the librarian of the Resource Center at DAU.  
The interview aimed to understand challenges, opportunities, and expectations related to an online bookstore system.

## Key Questions Asked
1. How do you think the Resource Center will benefit from an online bookstore?  
2. What problems do readers face when borrowing or finding books?  
3. What problems does the Resource Center face while maintaining books?  
4. What features would you like to see implemented in an online bookstore?  
5. What relevance do you think AI has in a library or bookstore?  
   - How can it help readers?  
   - What repetitive tasks can be automated?  
6. What changes have you observed in the reading habits of readers in the past few years?
